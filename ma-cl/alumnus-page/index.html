MA in CL: Alumus Page

This page is for current and past students on the MA in Computational Linguistics at Essex, and those who have taken courses with them. The original idea was to help them keep in touch with each other and with people who are still here.

Of course, the information may be wrong -- especially, it may be out of date.

To avoid spam, email addresses are in the form: 

person#address
replace the `#' by `@' to get the real address.

If you want me to post news about you, or put a link to your current email or webpage here, mail me: doug#essex.ac.uk.

Update 2016: I set this page sometime around 1995 (about 20 years ago!), long before things like Facebook and things were available, and it is probably not useful anymore. I'm pretty lazy about up-dating it.

Moreover, the degree was discontinued in 2010: the short version of the story is that I became Head of Department in 2010, and was no longer able to teach the core modules, and when I stopped being Head three years later, we realised that demand for the MA had not been strong for quite a while, and there were plenty of other things I could be teaching, so we did not re-instate it.
