On the Web

On the web, I have been the author/maintainer of a number of pages, including:

  • LaTeX for Linguists.
  • Lexical Functional Grammar (I maintained it for many years while it was hosted at Essex; it moved to its current home in Konstanz in 2019).
  • Head Driven Phrase Structure Grammar (I maintained it for many years while it was at Essex; it moved to Berlin in 2018).
  • CoBaLiSE which used to organize meetings for people interested in Constraint Based Linguistics in the SE of England.
  • CL Group The Constraint Based Linguistics Group at Essex; (see also Language and Computation Group WebPage, and the Language and Computation Group Wiki; and also and the webpage for the Flatlands Workshop).
  • CL/MT research group at Essex.
  • The UCGAL (University Council of General and Applied Linguistics) webpage while it was at Essex. It is now here.
  • W3Corpora: WWW access to Corpora.
  • The CL/MT On-Line Bibliographies.
  • ACL Bibliographies
  • The Linguistics Association of Great Britain (LAGB) web site, and the LAGB Mailing List.
  • A `notice board' of job adverts, conference announcements, etc., but I stopped in 2004 (so it has only historic interest).
  • An extensive list of `other sites' of linguistic interest, but I stopped some years ago, because the search engines got to be so good (so it has only historic interest).
